Invisible Friends Room


Fan-art of one of my favorite NFT sets.
Modeled with Maya, Blender and Zbrush. Layout and Render in Blender.
The Desk, PC and Chair are models from Sketchfab.
The intention was to create a room of one of the invisible friends with some of the best traits of the NFT set.
