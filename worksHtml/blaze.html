Blaze - Supercell Contest


This was our entry for the Fairy Town Supercell Challenge.
Modeled in Maya and Zbrush, textured in Substance Painter, rigged in Maya and rendered in Blender Cycles.
The goal was to create a character based on one of the Supercell games with the theme Fairy Town, so after some creative process we decided to create a fire dragon prince that captures the essence of Brawl Stars. If you wish to see more developing on the concept go to my partner post.
