About Concept Art Work

Monsieur Fernand


I sculpted the mafia guy model based on Marc Peyronen's concept.
Sculpted in Zbrush, XGen Gromming, procedurally textured and rendered in Arnold. Photoshop for compositing.
My goal was to achieve a film noir mood in the piece and learn XGen.
